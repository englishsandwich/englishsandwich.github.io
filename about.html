Credits & Sources

This game was inspired by food conversations on our stream and by our international Twitch chat community! We also got inspiration from Good Mythical Morning's International Taste Test segment.

The code is built with help from CodeExplained.org and Web Dev Simplified. Thanks!

Please note that not all locations listed in this game are soverign nations recognized by the United Nations or the international community. This is not a political statement, but an attempt to showcase the diversity of cultures around the world. Additionally, some dishes are common across regions, because national borders often do not accurately reflect ethnic or cultural borders. We attempted to elaborate on these nuances throughout the game.
If you see any discrepancies, have a comment, or want to add a dish, please contact us!
